Our Mission

Religious practices help Cybersecurity Incident Responders cope with trauma developed within the profession. This website aims to provide support by sharing faith-based strategies and personal stories of healing.

Why This Matters

Cybersecurity responders face constant threats, long hours, and emotional strain. The integration of theology offers a unique approach to healing by making connections between the two discipline through belonging, believing, and doing.
